  • How do I change my username?

    Head to the forums, then click your username on the top right of the page. Once you do this multiple options will pop up, press "Change username", input your new username in the textbox, and then press save. This can be done once every thirty days.

  • My account is pending verifcation and i'm not recieving an email?

    First ensure that your email is typed correctly, if it is then head to your email and be sure to check your spam/trash folder. If you still don't see it, head to discord for additional support.

  • Can I use another payment method to donate?

    The only accepted payment method for donations currently is paypal, you can also use a credit/debit through paypal to donate without creating an account. If paypal doesn't process your payment, you will have to contact paypal support.

  • Do different tiers of donations effect how fast an application is reviewed?

    No, all tiers are equal priority and will be reviewed prior to normal applications. The title you recieve does vary based on the donation.

  • Can I get more detail on the reasons my application was denied?

    Unfortuntely we do not provide additional detail besides the detail you recieve in your alerts.

  • I don't have permission to submit an application?

    First ensure that your discord and forum profile are connected, if you still cant submit a normal application you may have hit the 5 application cap and will need to donate to submit a donator application. If you have donated and cannot submit a donator application, first ensure that your donation has gone through paypal, if the funding source was a bank/echeck, paypal takes a while to process. This time can be found on the summary of the paypal transaction. If your paypal transaction has gone through and you still don't have donator tags, please create a support ticket and include a screenshot of your paypal transaction reciept as well as the transaction id. Support tickets can be created here.

  • How long does it take to process my application?

    The estimated time frame changes constantly, it is impossible to know. Asking this question in the support channels/as a support ticket will result in your application taking longer to process.

  • How do I know my application was reviewed?

    Once your application is reviewed it will be moved to closed applications, you will recieve an alert of why it was moved, which will state whether your app was denied/approved, if denied why it was denied. The alerts can be found here. You must be logged in to view them, and they dissapear after a while.
